About Me

I am currently a Senior at the University of California, Irvine, pursuing a B.S. in Computer Science and Engineering. My journey into technology has been shaped by a curiosity for problem-solving and a drive to create impactful solutions. Alongside my studies, I've explored diverse opportunities ranging from embedded systems development to web and UI/UX design.Originally, I began with community-oriented roles during the pandemic, teaching mathematics and programming to underserved communities. As my interest in technology deepened, I transitioned into more technical projects such as designing a Smart Mirror with voice-enabled controls, building a sleep quality monitoring system, and developing an AVR-based music player.
Whether managing marketing strategies at UCI or participating in hackathon projects, I strive to bring creativity, adaptability, and collaboration to every challenge. My multifaceted background reflects a commitment to blending technical expertise with user-focused innovation.

Currently, I am deeply immersed in several technical and leadership roles. As a Senior at the University of California, Irvine, I am actively collaborating on a Smart Mirror project, integrating voice control and real-time API data. Simultaneously, I am refining my work on a Sleep Quality Monitoring System using sensor-driven analytics and working on innovative embedded systems like an AVR-based music player.
Beyond academics, I stay engaged by contributing to UCI's community as a Student Coordinator for Arroyo Vista Housing, where I enhance operational efficiency through automation and accessibility-focused design. I also participate in hackathons, leveraging my coding expertise in Python, Java, and front-end technologies to create user-centric solutions. These experiences embody my passion for combining technology and creativity to make a meaningful impact.
